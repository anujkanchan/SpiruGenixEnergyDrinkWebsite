Energize Your Essence

Spirulina

Spirulina is a blue-green algae known for its exceptional nutritional value. Rich in plant-based protein, iron, and antioxidants, it helps boost energy, strengthen immunity, support muscle recovery, and detoxify the body.

Energy Boost

Natural sources like spirulina and Vitamin B12 support steady energy by enhancing metabolism and reducing fatigue. They provide lasting vitality without sugar or caffeine.

Vitamin B-12

Vitamin B12 is vital for red blood cell production, nerve health, and DNA synthesis. It supports energy levels and is especially important for vegetarians and vegans.

Our Achievements & Mission

WePitch 1.0 Achievement

KGROUPINDIA proudly secured the 4th Position (3rd Runner-up) among the Top 6 finalists at the WePitch 1.0 – Student Startup Innovation event, hosted by MIT ADT University India in collaboration with SOC’s Entrepreneurship Cell & CRiEYA.

Competing among 30 teams, we narrowly missed the Top 3 by just 2 points! Our idea, SpiruGenix – India’s First B12-Boosting Lemon Drink, was recognized for its innovation, health benefits, and entrepreneurial potential.

A special thanks to Our Mentor: Prof. Sumit Chuttar – Founder of 108 Springboard for their invaluable support.
